02 — About

A UX designer, and a photographer at heart.

These days my focus is UX design — shaping user-centred products at Centraal Beheer. I came to it through front-end development, building learning-analytics and marketplace interfaces, which still grounds how I design.

My background in Open-ICT and a photojournalism minor taught me to care equally about how something works and how it looks. Along the way I chaired study association Indicium through its first lustrum year, mentored students, and reviewed code.

Away from screens I shoot event and documentary photography, including for the Nederlands Vestingmuseum.
